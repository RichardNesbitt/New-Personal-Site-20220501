Obsessed with Accessibility

Before I knew what accessibility was, I was aware that my dad (partially color-blind) didn't see my web projects the same way that everyone else did, and that was a driving force in my early studies on accessibility.

My professional journey with Accessibility began in 2018 at Arizona State University evaluating their in-house platforms and 3rd party platforms with the focus of remediating accessibility issues.
